Description:

The target audience will include ages 18-70 years old that have access to a computer that live in or are visiting the city of Orlando.

Personas:

Parents that are getting their getting their kids ready for school and are wondering how to dress them in Orlando.
People going to DisneyWorld that would like to dress properly for their day adventure at the parks.

Scenarios:

  • Mother getting child ready for school in the morning and checks the weather to see if it will rain later in the day.
  • A family getting ready to go to the parks and see if it will be hot so they can wear shorts and tank tops.
  • Checking weather to see if extra sunscreen is needed for the Disney Parks
  • Family checking to see if their Disney trip will be ruined by rain or if they need to bring more water for the hotter days.
  • Mother checking weather to see if she needs to bundle up or stay cool before stepping outside and rushing to take her kids to school.
